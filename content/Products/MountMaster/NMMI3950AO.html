Features:

• Supplied with 3/8” mount
• 1 inch matte black alloy body tube
• AO models feature parallax adjustment on bell 10yds to infinity
• IR models feature illuminated reticle red/green
• HMD reticles
• Multicoated lenses
• Waterproof, fogproof, shockproof
• Nitrogen filled
• 1/4 MOA adjustments (7mm@100m)
• Fast eyefocus
