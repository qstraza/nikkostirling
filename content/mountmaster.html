MOUNTMASTER

 

Vsi od naših preizkušenih in dokazanih Mountmaster daljnogledov so cenovno konkurenčni in dovolj robustni da se zoperstavijo vsemu. Ponujajo 1/4 '' turrete ki jih lahkABOUT UStavljamo s prsti, 3/8 '' match montaže ter lepo sliko. Na voljo z osvetljenim križem (IR) ali brez, prav tako pa ponujajABOUT UStavitev paralakse (AO). Vsi Mountmaster daljnogledi imajo multi premaz na lečah, so testirani za trzaje in napolnjeni z dušikom, zato so popolnoma odporni na vodo in meglo. Vse to skupaj zagotavlja pravi performans tako na lovu kot na strelišču.

MOUNTMASTER

 

All our tried and proven Mountmaster scope series are competitively priced and are rugged engouh to stand up the real us. Featuring 1/4” finger adjustable turrets and the option of an accurate 3/8” match or standard mount. Available with or without illuminated retilce (IR) and prallaxe adjustment on the bell (AO) or even as classic scope just with mount. All Mountmaster scopes feature fully multicoated lenses, are shock tested and dry-nitrogen purged at the factory to make them fully water and fog proof for guaranteed performance, whether you are on a hunt or at the range.
